About the Group

The Bistoni Group develops and applies advanced quantum chemical methods—multireference approaches, DFT, and local correlation—to study catalytic mechanisms, noncovalent interactions, and biomolecular systems in the gas and solution phase.

Publications

2025 – Dissecting London dispersion with quantum chemistry. ACS Central Science
This study introduces a new method for breaking down dispersion forces into atom-specific contributions, enhancing interpretability of intermolecular interactions.
2024 – Local energy decomposition of dispersion effects. Acc. Chem. Res.
A novel decomposition scheme reveals the contribution of dispersion to chemical bonding and reactivity in organometallic systems.
